Startup Growth Lite

Startup Growth Lite for Drupal by More than (just) Themes is based on our Premium Startup Growth theme.

Startup Growth Lite has been ported to Drupal and is supported by More than (just) Themes, as part of our ongoing effort to bring top quality themes to the Drupal community.

There are two versions of Startup Growth Lite; a version for Drupal 7, and another one for Drupal 8. Feel welcome to grab the one you're interested in.

To stay tuned with new theme releases, updates to existing themes, offers and other goodies follow us on Twitter or like us on Facebook. Check out our blog too.
